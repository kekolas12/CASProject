#TR
Business katmaný httpGet,httpPut, httpPost, httpDelete kýsýmlarý için gerekli olan entitiy leri içeren 
kod parçasý. Bunlar Request ve Response olarak management katmanlarýnda ayrýldý.


#EN 
The Business layer is the code segment that contains the entities required for 
httpGet, httpPut, httpPost, and httpDelete. These are separated as Request and Response in the management layers.
